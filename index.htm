Navigating the Data

This map showcases four categories of projects in the DVRPC region that reduce energy use and greenhouse gas emissions. Clicking on a project will display detailed information, pictures, and some of the ways projects are reducing energy use and greenhouse gas emissions.

DVRPC is actively seeking additions to this map! Please contact Adam Beam ( abeam@dvrpc.org ) if you are aware of a project to be considered for inclusion. In addition, please contact Adam if you are the owner or operator of a project on this map and would like to correct or update information, or if you would like the project removed.
